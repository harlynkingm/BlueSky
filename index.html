Why Blue Sky?

Why did we pick the name 'Blue Sky?' It's our favorite place to be.

In the air, on the water, on a golf course, or on the beach- we always hope for a Blue Sky. We want our division leaders to operate in the same way-

We want all of our employees to be valued and to share the same vision.

Our Promise

Respect is priority number one- through the way we do business, conduct ourselves, and how we treat others.

Word is Bond

We only offer valuations we can close on. No bait and switching. Our word is our bond.

Speed to Close

We demand a streamlined, user-friendly approach with due diligence and legal documentation. Our lawyers, bankers, and accountants are valued partners and share similar visions to that of Blue Sky.

No Beauracracy

We believe in moving fast- using the information and goals of each party to find a common, closable solution.

Everyone Wins

We are firm believers that both sides can win. Buyer and seller. Customer and vendor. We want to make Blue Sky a unique experience in a crowded, sometimes difficult world.
